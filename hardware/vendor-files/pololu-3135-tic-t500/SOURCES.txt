Pololu Tic T500 USB Multi-Interface Stepper Motor Controller (#3135)
====================================================================

BOM item 11 (default driver) in ../../vibration-motor-and-solenoid.md

Product page Resources tab (source for everything in this folder):
  https://www.pololu.com/product/3135/resources

Files
-----
cad/tic-stepper-motor-controller-models.zip
    Vendor ZIP bundle of STEP / STL models for the entire Tic family
    (T500, T834, T825, T249, 36v4, etc.). Use the T500-specific files
    inside; the others are kept for reference since the PCB outline is
    shared across most of the family.

dimensions/tic-t500-drill-guide.pdf
    Original Pololu T500 drill-guide PDF (already in this folder before
    the manual-download batch).

dimensions/tic-stepper-motor-controller-dimensions.pdf
    Full mechanical dimension diagram (covers the whole Tic family).

datasheets/tic.pdf
    Tic stepper-motor-controller user's guide (covers serial / I²C /
    USB protocol, Tic Control Center configuration, current-limit
    setup, etc. — same PDF as on the product Resources tab).
