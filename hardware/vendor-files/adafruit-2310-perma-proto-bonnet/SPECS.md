# Adafruit Perma-Proto Bonnet Mini Kit for Pi (#2310) — dimensions

Reproduced verbatim from the Adafruit product page so we can author
rough CAD without re-fetching:
<https://www.adafruit.com/product/2310>

```
Header Dimensions: 5 mm × 51 mm × 12 mm  (0.2" × 2"   × 0.5")
PCB Dimensions:   66 mm × 57 mm × 2 mm   (2.6" × 2.2" × 0.1")
```

These are the headline numbers we use when laying out the auger-channel
chassis around the Pi Zero 2 W + Bonnet stack. The PCB outline matches
the Pi Zero 2 W footprint (65 × 30 mm) extended outward over the 2×20
header to ~57 mm wide; the 5 × 51 × 12 mm "header dimensions" describe
the female 2×20 socket that mates with the Pi.
