Adafruit Perma-Proto Bonnet Mini Kit for Pi (#2310) — supplemental files
========================================================================

BOM item 6 in ../../vibration-motor-and-solenoid.md

This folder collects extra design files manually downloaded from the
Adafruit "Downloads" pages around the Perma-Proto Bonnet family. The
canonical Eagle PCB files for the #2310 Bonnet itself live in `pcb/`
(mirrored from github.com/adafruit/Adafruit-Perma-Proto-Bonnet-PCB);
the additional files here are:

pcb-hat-variant/
  "Adafruit HaD Proto HAT rev A.sch"   # Eagle schematic of the Adafruit HaD Proto HAT (full-size HAT
  "Adafruit HaD Proto HAT rev A.brd"   # PCB variant of the same Perma-Proto idea — useful as a Pi-HAT
  LICENSE.txt                          # mechanical envelope reference). CC BY-SA 3.0.
                                       # Source: github.com/adafruit/Adafruit-HaD-Proto-HAT-PCB
                                       # (linked from the #2310 product Downloads section).
fritzing/
  "Adafruit Perma-Proto HAT.fzpz"      # Fritzing part for the Perma-Proto HAT family — drop-in for
                                       # quick wiring sketches in Fritzing.
                                       # Source: linked from the Adafruit "Fritzing parts" library.

License: CC BY-SA 3.0 (per pcb-hat-variant/LICENSE.txt for the Eagle
files). The Fritzing `.fzpz` is published by Adafruit under the same
CC BY-SA 3.0 terms as the rest of their Fritzing parts library.

See also SPECS.md in this folder for the headline mechanical dimensions
copied verbatim from the Adafruit #2310 product page.
