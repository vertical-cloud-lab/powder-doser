StepperOnline does not publish a STEP model on the product page; for
mechanical fit-up against the 11HS18-0674S (5 mm D-cut shaft) a simple
Ø18 × 25 mm cylinder with two 5 mm bores is sufficient. See the parent
README for the manual-download caveat.
