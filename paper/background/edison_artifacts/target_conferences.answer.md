## Strategic Recommendations

1. **Lead with SDL-focused venues.** The Accelerate Conference and the ORNL SDL workshop series are the most natural homes for the integrated hardware + autonomous-experimentation story. These communities explicitly value open-hardware, user-developed automation infrastructure, and practical SDL building blocks (pelkie2025democratizingselfdrivinglabs pages 1-3, silva2024shapingthefuture pages 1-4).

2. **Target MRS Fall and SFF for materials and AM credibility.** For peer recognition in materials science and additive manufacturing, an MRS Fall symposium on autonomous experimentation or an SFF paper on the AM hardware side provides high visibility and archival value (sperry2023formationofeasytoremove pages 15-15).

3. **Use ASME IDETC/CIE for the generative-CAD angle.** The DAC and CIE tracks within IDETC/CIE are the most appropriate archival engineering-design venues for the LLM/code-based generative-CAD pipeline (seng2025detc2025168839 pages 1-2). Recent work on AI copilots for design, CAD-prompted generative models, and generative statistically self-similar structures has appeared here.

4. **Consider Faraday Discussions for a polished, high-prestige presentation.** If a future Faraday Discussion is announced on automation, AI for materials, or data-driven discovery, the project would fit well as a pre-submitted paper emphasizing the closed-loop calibration and autonomous discovery infrastructure (cooper2025concludingremarksfaraday pages 1-3, cooper2025concludingremarksfaraday pages 23-26).

5. **Use AI4Mat workshop "Tools & Findings" tracks for ML-community visibility.** The AI4Mat workshops at NeurIPS and ICLR have specific tracks for tools, infrastructure, and findings that are more accommodating of hardware-rich contributions than typical ML venues (miret2026ai4maticlr2026iclr2026 pages 4-6, miret2026ai4maticlr2026iclr2026 pages 1-2, miret2026ai4maticlr2026iclr2026 pages 2-4).

6. **GOSH for community building and adoption.** While GOSH does not produce archival papers, it is unmatched for building a user community around the open-hardware doser design and getting feedback on documentation, reproducibility, and accessibility (arancio2021openingupthe pages 15-17, arancio2021openingupthe pages 10-12).

7. **Stretch ML/graphics venues only with a strong standalone ML contribution.** CVPR, SIGGRAPH, NeurIPS, and ICLR main conferences are realistic only if the generative-CAD pipeline yields novel ML methodology that stands on its own. For LLM-based CAD generation, CVPR workshops and AAAI have been receptive (chen2026learningfromdesign pages 9-11, usama2026nurbgenhighfidelitytexttocad pages 7-8, li2025cadllamaleveraginglarge pages 1-2).
