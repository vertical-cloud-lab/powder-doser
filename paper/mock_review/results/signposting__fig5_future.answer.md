## Verdict on AI-vs-human signposting in Fig. 5 (future work)

**The current figure and caption do not signpost AI vs human contributions at all.** They describe geometry only, with no statement of who/what produced either panel. That directly violates the explicit reviewer instruction at `paper/main.tex` line 189 (PR #97, `pr97_comments.md`): *"Throughout this section, clarify where ai was used. This section and its figures will be changed as updated information is uploaded."* — i.e., the figure is a placeholder, and that status is not currently disclosed.

By contrast, the Fig. 2 region of the manuscript already names the AI workflow explicitly ("LLM coding agents … build123d/CadQuery … OpenSCAD … Zoo Design Studio … Zookeeper", main.pdf p.3). Fig. 5's caption is the only figure caption that drops this signposting entirely.
