Design a 20-tooth spur gear servo pinion that presses onto an MG996R servo
spline. All units millimetres. Orient the gear axis along Z, teeth flat in the
print plane.

Spur gear: module 0.9083, 20 teeth, 20 degree pressure angle, pitch diameter
18.2, tip diameter 20.2.

Central bore: diameter 6.0 with a single flat (a chordal flat cut on one side)
to engage the MG996R 25-tooth output spline, running through the gear.

Add an M3 countersink on the top face concentric with the bore for the servo
horn retaining screw.

Why it matters: each of the two servos drives a 40-tooth mounting-plate hinge
gear through this 20-tooth pinion, giving a 2:1 reduction for finer, stronger
tilt control.
