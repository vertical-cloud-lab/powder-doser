Design a small bracket plate that holds the angular position of a tap collar
on a powder doser. All units millimetres. It bolts flat onto a base plate in
the XY plane with the auger axis running along Y above it.

Base tab: a flat rectangular plate about 40 wide (X) by 20 deep (Y) by 6 thick
(Z), with two M3 clearance holes (diameter 3.4) for bolting it down.

Hardstop post: a vertical post or wall rising about 25 mm from the plate top
near one edge, positioned to catch the tap collar's hardstop ear and arrest
its rotation, so the collar cannot spin with the turning auger and tangle its
wires.

Why it matters: this mount holds the tap collar's angular position via a
positive hardstop so the collar cannot rotate with the auger and wind up its
motor and solenoid wires.
