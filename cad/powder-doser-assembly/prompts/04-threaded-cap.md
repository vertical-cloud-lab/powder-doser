Design a screw-on sealing cap (bottle-cap style) for a threaded powder auger.
All units millimetres. Orient the open end up, print-upright.

The cap is a closed cylindrical cup with an internal single-start screw
thread that mates with an external auger thread of major radius 12.5.

Internal thread: pitch 4.0 mm per turn, thread depth 1.0, grown radially by
0.35 mm clearance for a hand-tight fit (so internal minor radius about 12.85,
major radius about 11.85), tooth half angle 58 degrees, right-handed. The
thread is on the INSIDE wall of the cup only.

Cup wall thickness 3.0, solid closed top 3.0 thick, with about 3.0 mm of
clearance space above the engaged threads. Overall outer diameter about 31.7,
overall height about 31.0. Add a 1.5 mm by 45 degree chamfer on the top outer
edge.

Why it matters: this cap screws onto the threaded auger to seal a pre-filled
powder cartridge; the 0.35 mm thread clearance gives a smooth hand-screw fit.
