A flat back-plate bracket for a Raspberry Pi Zero 2 W. Plate outline 70 mm × 32 mm × 3 mm. Four M2.5 stand-off bosses 4 mm tall and 5 mm diameter, located on the official Pi Zero 2 W 58 mm × 23 mm rectangular hole pattern, centered on the plate. Two slot mounting holes (4 mm wide, 12 mm long) at each short end of the plate, on the long-axis centerline, for chassis attachment. Add a 16 mm × 8 mm rectangular cable cut-out centered on one long edge for the GPIO ribbon. Do NOT model the Raspberry Pi itself. Do NOT add a lid or cover.
